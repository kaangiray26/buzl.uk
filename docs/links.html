Links

Here are some websites and content I like from around the web.

  • Hacker News - Everything related but not limited to computer science and interesting things that are worth sharing
  • GamingOnLinux - About gaming on linux but also includes news about game releases, game development and gaming software
  • OMG! Linux - A blog about linux and open source software
  • LOW←TECH MAGAZINE - A magazine on forgotten and obsolete technologies, running on solar power
  • Quanta Magazine - News and articles about science
  • Darknet Diaries - A podcast about real hacking stories and hidden sights of the internet
  • Indie Hackers - A nice website to get insights on how other people create projects and profitable business plans
  • 0x00sec - An online community about cybersecurity
  • It’s FOSS News - About FOSS and Linux
  • Phrack - Highly influential e-zine about hackers, cybersecurity and internet culture
  • Bear Blog - Discover new posts from the smallest blogging platform on the web
  • Click me - for a random article
  • ruffle - An open source Flash Player emulator to play your old nostalgic games
