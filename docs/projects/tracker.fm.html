Tracker.fm

~

See what your Last.fm friends are listening to in real-time.

This is just a small application without any authorization to check out which songs your Last.fm friends are listening to at the moment.

You can see their last listened songs or you can see what they are listening at the moment, indicated with a small "Live" mark on the bottom right corner of the album cover.

There is also a timer function, which refreshes the scrobbles periodically.

Check out the online instance and the repo !
