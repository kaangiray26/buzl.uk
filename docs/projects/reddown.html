Client-side Reddit Media Downloader

I know, there are lots of downloader websites out there. I used to have a downloader bot in Reddit too. But it was working on the server-side and used to get broken too often.

After running it for a while, I came up with the idea of running everything on the client-side. So, here's that!

Once you open the website, you can enter either a submission id, a submission url or a submission permalink to download images and videos.

You can also use the search parameter id followed by the Base36 submission id to download the content automatically once you load the site.

Check the website yourself !
